About AAA --

AAA started its operations in 1998 to impart and promote aviation training courses approved by Directorate General of Civil Aviation, Civil Aviation Department, Govt of India. The organization was started by Capt Shreya, who went on to become the first lady Chief Flying Instructor of India in the year 2012. AAA is one of the only few recognized institutes that has sustained the dynamics of economic boom and recession alike and continues to operate in the state capital of Mumbai.
